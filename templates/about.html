About Mongo Quiz

The quick and easy way to create quizzes for friends and family

Mongo Quiz is designed as a bit of social fun to be played among friends and family. It is played in real-time, meaning everyone plays at the same time. We suggest using two devices, one to allow the Quiz Master and players to communicate with each other (using platforms* such as Zoom, Skype or Hangouts), and a second where the game is played.

The Quiz Master creates the quiz, deciding on how many rounds, the subject of each, the number of questions per round, and the difficulty of each question. When all players are ready, the Quiz Master publishes the quiz and begins to launch/ask the questions one by one. As the questions are asked, they become visible to each player. You will be given 4 possible options and 20 seconds to answer. When everyone has answered the question, the Quiz Master will launch the next, and so on until all questions have been asked and rounds played.

At the end of each round the Quiz Master will publish the scores. Players will be able to see where they rank in each round and overall, as well as seeing everyoneâ€™s scores.
