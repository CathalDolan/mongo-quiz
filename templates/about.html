About Mongo Quiz

The BIG fun quiz game to play alone or with family and friends.

Mongo Quiz ws born out of our weekly family quiz during Covid-19 lockdown. With family members spread across the country, our weekly quiz was a great way to stay in touch. Unfortunately however, it petered out after a few months because putting the Quiz togethertok too much me.

The idea of Mongo Quiz is that it's run like a tyical fun quiz with a quiz master asking the questions, and each team correcng their own answers nd submitting their scores at the end of each round. We've just simplifed it be compiling the quesions for you, and helping each tam keep track of thei scores.

For the best fun (and to help prevent cheating 😀), the game is best played in conjunction with an app like Zoom* or Hangouts, where teams can share their screens as well as interact between rounds. Just make sure to turn the volume down when conerring between team members on the answers!
